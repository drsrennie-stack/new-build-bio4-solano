How the boost works

The cumulative exam can lift your lowest TBL

If you fill in your lowest individual TBL score and your cumulative lecture exam is higher than it, your TBL component is recomputed with that lowest TBL replaced. The boost only ever helps, never hurts.

Reweighting blank fields

The math adjusts to what you have

Leave a field blank and that component is excluded from the average; the remaining weights are scaled so they still sum to 100%. Early in the term, this gives you a sensible running estimate.
