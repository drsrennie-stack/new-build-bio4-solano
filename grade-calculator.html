How the boost works

A strong cumulative lifts your lowest TBL

The calculator finds your lowest entered TBL automatically (highlighted in gold). If your cumulative lecture exam is higher than that TBL, that one TBL is replaced by the cumulative for the average (highlighted in rust). The boost only ever helps; it never lowers a score.

Running while empty

The calculator works mid-term too

Leave any field blank and that score is excluded; the weights reweight to what you have entered so far. Early in the term, this gives you a sensible projected standing. As you fill in scores, the estimate sharpens.
