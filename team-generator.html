How the teams were built (transparency report)

Students are sorted by background strength and dealt across teams in a snake pattern so every team gets a comparable spread. The tool then swaps members to guarantee each team has at least one organizer and one tech-comfortable member, to even out work-hours load, to mix introverts and extroverts, to share at least one meeting window, and to avoid leaving anyone as the lone member of a group. Each team card shows its resulting composition.
