How hosting works.
  • When you offer to host, you run the session yourself, on your own Zoom or in person. If you host on Zoom, record it; that recording is what earns you host credit. For in-person sessions, type the location instead of a link.
  • After the session, click "Host? Add recording and attendance" on your session. Paste your recording link and your Zoom attendance report. That submits everyone's attendance for tracking and shares the replay.
  • When you sign up, use your full name as it appears on the class roster, and use that same name in Zoom, so the host can match you in the attendance report.
  • We collect emails only to match people to sessions and verify hosts. Emails and attendance reports are private to Dr. Rennie, never posted on this page.
  • Be respectful of classmates' time. If you sign up, show up, or cancel your seat so someone else can take it.
