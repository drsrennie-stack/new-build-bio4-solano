Put Recall Rx on your phone

Your daily anatomy review, one tap away. Point your camera at the code, then add it to your home screen so it opens like an app.

Scan to open

Open your phone camera and hold it over the code. Tap the link that pops up. No app store, no login.

https://drsrennie-stack.github.io/new-build-bio4-solano/bio004-spaced-recall.html

iPhone or iPad

  1. Scan the code and open the link (Chrome or Safari are both fine).
  2. Tap the Share button, the box with an up arrow. In Chrome it is up in the address bar; in Safari it is at the bottom.
  3. Tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add. The Recall Rx icon appears on your home screen.

Android phone or computer

  1. Scan or open the link in Chrome.
  2. Phone: tap the three-dot menu, then Install app.
  3. Computer: click the Install icon in the address bar (a small screen with a down arrow).
  4. Confirm. The Recall Rx icon appears with your apps.
Pick one device and stick with it. Your progress, streak, and any cards you make are saved on that phone only, in that browser. Study in the same place each day, and save your weekly report as a PDF to turn in for your bonus.
