Use the same device and browser

Your progress, streak, and your own cards are saved only in this browser on this device. Nothing is uploaded. Pick one place to study, your laptop, your phone, or your tablet, and always come back to it in the same browser. If you switch devices or clear your browser data, your progress will not follow you. Put Recall Rx on your phone

Recall Rx installs like an app, no app store needed.

iPhone or iPad: tap the Share button (a box with an up arrow). In Chrome it is up in the address bar; in Safari it is at the bottom of the screen. Then tap "Add to Home Screen," then Add.

Android phone: open in Chrome, tap the three-dot menu, then "Install app."

Computer: in Chrome, click the Install icon in the address bar (a small screen with a down arrow).

Remember this becomes its own device. Whatever you study there stays there, so study in one place.

Reset

Erase all of your progress and your own cards on this device. This cannot be undone.
