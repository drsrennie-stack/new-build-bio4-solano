Competency Map

Not chapters. Measurable competencies you can prove, one at a time.

These competencies are the majority of what the course covers, and working through them honestly and consistently is very likely to be effective. No tool can promise a grade, though. Your result also depends on how much time you put in, how honest you are in your own self-assessment, exam-day factors like anxiety, and other things beyond this app. Use it alongside class, the structure list, and the syllabus.

Study Together

Mastery is social. Compete, cooperate, teach, and race. Working single-device prototypes built on your own question bank.

Mastery OS is a study aid, not a substitute for your lectures, labs, the structure list, or the syllabus. It cannot cover everything you are responsible for, and using it does not guarantee any particular grade. Your results depend on many factors, some of them unrelated to this tool. Always study from your primary course materials as well.
