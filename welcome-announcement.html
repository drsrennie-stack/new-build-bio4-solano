Welcome to BIO 004 Human Anatomy

Course Welcome and Weekly Rhythm

Hi everyone, welcome to the course. Before we meet, spend some time poking around the course site. Start with the homepage, then take a look at the syllabus and the schedule so you get a feel for how everything fits together and how to find your way around.

A couple of things that make this course a little different. I've written all the material myself, so there's no textbook to buy. Lectures are online and they're video, so you'll watch them and do the pre-work at night, then come to class ready to build on it the next day.

How the week works

You'll have a lab exam every Monday and a graded team-based learning session every Thursday. The one exception is the very end of the course: the last day of class is a Friday, and that final lab exam will fall on that Friday. And to be clear, there's no lab exam tomorrow since it's our first day.

The exam days and the TBL days are fixed and won't move. Everything else can shift. As we move through the material, we may need an extra day on something or have to make up time somewhere, so we'll stay flexible. All of the material stays open all week, so you can work at your own pace.
